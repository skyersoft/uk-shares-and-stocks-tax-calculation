## ðŸ’± Currency Exchange Rules

### Capital Gains
- **Acquisition Cost**: Convert using exchange rate on purchase date
- **Disposal Proceeds**: Convert using exchange rate on sale date
- **FX Gains/Losses**: Included in capital gain calculation

### Dividends
- **Payment Date**: Convert using exchange rate on dividend payment date
- **Withholding Tax**: Converted at same rate as dividend

### Currency Holdings
- Generally not subject to CGT for individuals
- Business context may create taxable FX gains/losses
- Small amounts may be exempt under de minimis rules

### Tax Year Handling
- Calculations must be performed separately for each UK tax year
- Losses can be carried forward between tax years

### Rounding Rules
- All amounts rounded to 2 decimal places (pence)

### Validation Rules
- Disposal dates must be after acquisition dates
- Cannot sell more shares than owned (including pooled shares)
- Matching rules applied in strict priority order
