Are you aware that you can invest up to £20,000 every tax year without paying a single penny in Capital Gains Tax or Income Tax on the returns? A Stocks and Shares ISA is essential for any UK investor.

{{youtube:vmMJ1tzqNkE}}

### What is a Stocks & Shares ISA?

A Stocks and Shares ISA is a "tax wrapper" that you put your investments inside. Any profits you make from rising share prices, and any dividends you receive, are completely shielded from HM Revenue & Customs. In a landscape where the standard Annual Exempt Amount (AEA) for Capital Gains Tax has been drastically reduced, maximizing your ISA allowance is more critical than ever.

### Setting Up and Brokers

Many major UK brokers support Stocks & Shares ISAs. As covered in this video by Toby Newbatt:
*   **Trading 212** offers a very popular, commission-free Stocks and Shares ISA.
*   **InvestEngine** specializes in ETF portfolios with no management fees for DIY investors.
*   Other established brokers such as Hargreaves Lansdown or Vanguard also offer robust ISA accounts perfectly suited to long-term tax-exempt investing.
