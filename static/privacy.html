Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Transaction data from uploaded CSV or QFX files
  • Tax year selections and calculation preferences
  • Usage analytics to improve our service
  • Browser and device information for optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Perform tax calculations and generate reports
  • Improve our calculation algorithms and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations and tax regulations
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is processed in secure AWS Lambda functions with encryption
  • Files are processed in memory only - no permanent storage
  • Temporary files are automatically deleted after processing
  • All communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access controls and monitoring for all system components
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

4. Data Retention and Storage

No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your transaction data. All uploaded files and calculation results are:

  • Processed entirely in memory during your session
  • Automatically deleted within minutes of processing
  • Never stored on persistent storage systems
  • Not cached or backed up anywhere

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • AWS Lambda & CloudFront: For secure computation and content delivery
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements (uses cookies)
  • Amazon Associates: For affiliate product recommendations
  • Bootstrap CDN & Font Awesome: For UI components (no data collection)

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your calculation preferences (local storage only)
  • Analyze website usage patterns (anonymized data)
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Improve website performance and user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain data processing

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your home country, including:

  • AWS data centers in the EU, UK, and US regions
  • All transfers comply with GDPR adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place for data protection

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last updated" date
  • Notify users of significant changes via website notice

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Website: Through our contact form
  • Data Protection: For GDPR-related inquiries
  • Security Issues: Report via our security contact
Key Privacy Highlights
  • Zero permanent storage of your financial data
  • In-memory processing only during calculations
  • Automatic deletion of all uploaded files
  • Enterprise-grade security throughout the process
